Vanguard OS v2.0

The Operator Guide.

Step-by-step instructions for managing Hajj & Umrah logistics in Ahmedabad.

Core Operating Procedures

Phase 01

Registry Creation

Click on + New Entry from your dashboard. Our system uses a Dynamic Manifest logic.

  • Multi-PAX Support: Use the "+ Add Traveler" button to bundle families into one reference ID.
  • Live Preview: As you type the customer name, the sidebar "Preview" updates in real-time.
  • Auto-ID: The system automatically assigns a company-prefixed UID (e.g., KS-001).
Phase 02

Smart Financials

Vanguard OS automates your bookkeeping. Use the Financial Clearance section to manage cash flow.

Live Balance

The UI calculates the balance instantly. Red text indicates an outstanding amount; Green indicates clearance.

Payment Stages

Select "Final" to automatically toggle the system status to PAID.

Phase 03

Digital Distribution

Once a record is saved, use the View action to access the professional voucher.

  • WhatsApp Share: Use the Apple-style SHARE button to send a live receipt link directly to the customer's phone.
  • Mobile PDF: Our receipts automatically reformats for mobile screens, acting as a digital e-ticket for pilgrims.

Advanced Dashboard Controls

Pulse Intelligence

The right-hand sidebar tracks every action. It logs who edited a record and when, providing total transparency.

Power Search

Search by Receipt Number, Customer Name, or Booking ID. Use filters to isolate "Pending" payments instantly.

FAQ & Support

How do I edit a mistake?
Locate the entry on the dashboard and click the icon. This opens the pre-filled form allowing you to update anything from Passport numbers to Payment stages.
Is the data secure?
Yes. Kakiwala & Sons data is isolated within your specific "Node". Operators from other companies cannot see your passenger manifest or financial data.
Can I export for audits?
Use the CSV button at the top of the dashboard to download your entire registry into a spreadsheet for local tax compliance in Ahmedabad.
